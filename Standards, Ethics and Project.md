# Standards, Ethics and Project
___
### Standards
-   Rules, guidelines and heuristics
	-   assist in achieving “good” practice   
	-   Not best practice

-   **De facto** - implicit agreement
	-   Easily changed

-   **De jure** - formal agreement
	-   Usually debated & documented

### Standards Adoption
-   Voluntary
	-   Achieving good practices
	-   Safety net (potentially legally)  

-   Required
	-   Demands of clients
	-   Certification requirements
	-   Follow on from other standards
	-   Process improvement activity

### SE Standards
-   Normative and informative
-   Document centered
-   Adaptable
